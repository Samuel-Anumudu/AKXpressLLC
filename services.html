WHERE CAN YOU DELIVER TO?

Arkansas is home & we are thrilled to structure our intrastate routes by supporting local businesses. However, we can service locally, regionally, or nationally! We can transport anywhere from 1200-1500 packages daily with 4-5 steady moving vehicles. Our cargo moves from Monday-Friday 5 am - 3 pm with a 24/7 hotline.

HOW DIVERSE IS YOUR FLEET?

We have cargo vans, sprinters, 12-16ft box trucks, 24-26ft straight trucks & even dually/flatbed for all of your heavy hotshot equipment. We’ll be open to gaining more routes involving furniture, pharmaceuticals, hospital organs, office equipment, government contracts which may consist of cement, mulch, motors, heavy equipment that doesn’t limit our Gross Vehicle Weight Rating.
